cheese Toast

Cheese toast boasts a deliciously comforting flavor profile, characterized by the rich, creamy notes of melted cheese that meld with the subtle, buttery richness of the bread's golden edges. This savory delight is often complemented by a medley of seasonings, such as garlic powder, paprika, or herbs, which add depth and complexity to the overall taste. Whether enjoyed on its own or customized with additional ingredients like sliced tomatoes or crispy bacon, cheese toast offers a satisfying blend of cheesy, savory, and buttery elements that make it an irresistible and quick-to-prepare treat.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : bread breakfast food

instructions

step 1

Warm the pan to a medium heat.

step 2

Toss the bread and cheese and roast to a needed level of crunch.

step 3

And the cheesy toast is ready to eat.
